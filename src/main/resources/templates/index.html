Welcome to Bank of Words

Bank of Words is a tool to help non-native speakers study and memorize foreign words. With our personal "bank of words," you can easily add new words and track your progress.

Features

  • Create a personal bank of words
  • Receive a set of words for repeating every day
  • Receive a set of new words from a book
  • Track personal statistics and progress

Get Started

To get started, simply create an account or log in to your existing account. From there, you can start adding new words and tracking your progress.
